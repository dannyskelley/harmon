Michigan Roofing Contractor — Licensed, Insured & Local

Infinity Home Solutions is one of Metro Detroit's most trusted roofing contractors. We've helped thousands of Michigan homeowners protect their homes with durable, high-quality roofing systems installed by experienced crews. Whether you need a complete roof replacement, storm damage repair, or a simple inspection, we're here to help — with honest pricing and a 10-year labor warranty.

Michigan weather is tough on roofs. Ice dams, heavy snow loads, hailstorms, and temperature extremes all take a toll. Our roofing systems are specifically chosen and installed to handle everything Michigan winters — and summers — throw at them.

Our Roofing Services

Full Roof Replacement

Complete tear-off and replacement of your existing roof with premium shingles or metal roofing. We remove all old materials, inspect and repair the decking, and install a new roof system built to last.

Roof Repair

Leaks, missing shingles, damaged flashing, or wind damage — our repair crews diagnose the problem and fix it right. We won't recommend more than you need.

Storm Damage Inspection

After severe weather, we offer honest, no-pressure storm damage assessments. We document damage clearly to support insurance claims and recommend the best path forward.

Metal Roofing

Long-lasting metal roofing systems that shed snow and ice efficiently. Ideal for Michigan homes looking for a 50+ year roofing solution with minimal maintenance.
