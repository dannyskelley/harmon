Frequently Asked Questions: Michigan Roof Replacement

How much does a new roof cost in Michigan?

Most roof replacements in Michigan range from $6,900 to $15,000 or more depending on roof size, pitch, and materials. We offer free, no-pressure estimates so you know exactly where you stand before committing to anything.

Do you offer financing for a roof replacement?

Yes. Financing is available for qualified Michigan homeowners. Ask about our financing options when you call for your free estimate at (866) 705-8388.

What shingle brands do you install?

We specialize in Atlas Roofing shingles: Pinnacle® Pristine, StormMaster® Shake, and Pinnacle® Sun. We also install Owens Corning Duration architectural shingles. We can bring color samples to your free estimate so you can see them on your home.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most Michigan home roofs are completed in 1 to 2 days. Larger or more complex roofs may take longer. We keep your property protected and clean up all debris and nails before we leave.

Do you work with insurance claims?

Yes. If your roof was damaged by a storm, we can provide a thorough inspection and document the damage clearly to support your insurance claim. We work with all major insurance carriers.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Metro Detroit, Southeast Michigan, and beyond, including Southgate, Wyandotte, Trenton, Taylor, Dearborn, Lincoln Park, Shelby Township, Midland, and surrounding communities.
