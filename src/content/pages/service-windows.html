Michigan Window Replacement: Energy-Efficient and Professionally Installed

Are your windows drafty, hard to open, foggy between the panes, or just old? Replacement windows from Infinity Home Solutions will transform how your home feels, warmer in winter, cooler in summer, quieter year-round, and more beautiful from the street. We offer a wide selection of window styles and glass options, all professionally installed and backed by a 10-year labor warranty.

Drafty windows are one of the leading causes of high energy bills in Michigan homes. Modern replacement windows with Low-E glass and argon gas fill dramatically reduce heat transfer, keeping your heated or cooled air inside where it belongs.

Window Styles We Install

Double-Hung Windows

The most popular choice for Michigan homes. Both sashes open for easy cleaning and excellent ventilation. Available in dozens of colors and grid patterns.

Casement Windows

Hinged on the side and opening outward with a crank handle. Casement windows provide excellent airflow and a tight seal when closed, great for energy efficiency.

Sliding Windows

Simple, low-maintenance windows that slide horizontally. Ideal for wide openings and spaces where you don't want outward projection.

Bay & Bow Windows

Add architectural character and natural light with a stunning bay or bow window. Great for living rooms and dining areas.

Picture Windows

Fixed windows that maximize views and natural light. Often combined with operable windows for ventilation.

Specialty & Custom Windows

Arched, geometric, and custom-shaped windows available to match your home's unique architecture.

What is Low-E glass and do I need it?

Low-E (Low Emissivity) glass has a microscopic coating that reflects heat. In Michigan's cold winters, it keeps heat inside. In summer, it reduces solar heat gain. For Michigan homes, Low-E glass is a worthwhile upgrade on any replacement window.
