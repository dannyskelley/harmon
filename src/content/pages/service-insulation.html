Our Insulation Services

Attic Insulation

Attic insulation is the single highest-impact upgrade most Michigan homes can make. We install blown-in and batt insulation to bring your attic to Michigan Energy Code standards and beyond. Proper attic insulation can reduce heating and cooling costs by 15 to 25%.

Wall Insulation

Exterior wall insulation dramatically reduces drafts and improves comfort throughout your home. We can install insulation in exterior walls during a siding project or as a standalone upgrade using blown-in techniques.

Crawl Space Insulation

Cold floors? Moisture issues? Crawl space insulation and vapor barriers keep your floors comfortable, prevent moisture damage, and improve indoor air quality. Essential for Michigan homes with crawl spaces.

Air Sealing

Air sealing goes hand-in-hand with insulation. We identify and seal gaps, cracks, and penetrations that allow conditioned air to escape and outside air to enter, multiplying the effectiveness of new insulation.

Insulation Removal

Old, damaged, or contaminated insulation removed safely and efficiently. We prep the space properly so new insulation performs at its best.

Insulation with Siding

Pairing new siding with insulated siding panels or wall insulation is one of the best ways to upgrade your home's energy performance in one project. Ask us about bundled pricing.

Frequently Asked Questions: Michigan Home Insulation

How do I know if my attic needs more insulation?

Signs include high heating and cooling bills, rooms that are hard to keep comfortable, drafts near exterior walls, ice dams on your roof in winter, or insulation that is thin, old, or visibly damaged. We offer free inspections to assess your home's insulation needs.

What R-value does Michigan require for attics?

Michigan Energy Code recommends R-49 to R-60 for attic insulation in most climate zones. Many older Michigan homes fall well short of this, costing homeowners significantly on heating bills each year.

Can insulation be added during a siding project?

Yes. Pairing new siding with insulated siding panels or wall insulation is a great way to improve your home's energy performance in a single project. Ask about bundled pricing when you call.
